My Favourite Application

Spotify

Spotify is a music streaming app that gives users access to millions of songs and podcasts. It provides personalized recommendations, curated playlists, and the ability to create and share playlists with friends. It's appreciated for its extensive music library and user-friendly interface.


Google Maps

Google Maps is a navigation app that offers detailed maps, real-time traffic updates, and turn-by-turn directions. It also provides information about local businesses, restaurants, and landmarks. Many people find it helpful for navigating and exploring new places.
